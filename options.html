YT Videos Hider - Options

You can add filters to hide certain videos from your Youtube subscriptions page. Enter here a list of criterias; if one of them matches a video, then that video will not be displayed.

Example: If you add "Syberia" in the Title match field, a video named "Syberia Let's Play - Episode 23" will not be displayed on your home page.

You can also specify an author for the filter. This way, if you enter "jeankevin95" in the Author field of our previous example, a video named "Syberia Let's Play - Episode 23" uploaded by the user "jeankevin95" WILL NOT be displayed, whereas a video named "5000 special: Syberia 3 review" uploaded by the user "schrodingerscatrules" WILL be displayed.

Be sure to click "save" when you're done, and enjoy! :)
