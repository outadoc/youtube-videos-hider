YT Videos Hider - Options

You can add filters to hide certain videos from your Youtube subscriptions page. Enter here a list of criterias; if one of them is found in the title of a video, then the video will not be displayed.

Example: If you add "Syberia", a video named "Syberia Let's Play - Episode 23" will not be displayed on your home page.

Enter one filter per line, and be sure to click "save" when you're done.
